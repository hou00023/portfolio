    Black & White Gallery

    I like wandering, sometimes sitting in a corner of Starbucks with my camera. If now I take out my camera and mark this moment in my eyes, the picture will look nice and vibrant. Chatting cheerfully, there are 5 people sitting in a round table in different colors. Yellow lights, brown tables, greyscale walls. Everything appears colorful and contrasting; they look pleasant to the eye and I feel the various vibrations right now. Now, I take a picture with my camera in monochromatic mode…

    Those pleasant colors are gone, lights are not yellow, tables are not brown, walls are darker than before… the image is no longer colorful. My eyes directly shift to see what the 5 people are doing, how the light from the window is falling on the table, and what the beautiful combination of different saturated monochrome colors adjust to each other… the image is simple and storytelling than before. For me it becomes a frame of precision and concision, a trick of lights and shadows, a game of people and their stories. And this, is what photography is to me.
